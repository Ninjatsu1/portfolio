Team

Why I started doing this?

I was inspired by my favourite game to make 3D turn based RPG game. I wanted to challenge myself and learn something new.

What I learnt

I learned to use sculpting in Blender when I made blue dog character.

I understood better how raycast works in Unity while I was making player character's movement and object selection in battlestate.

I learnt to use game states while I was making battlesystem. I learnt how to use coroutines while making it.

Next I drew two skill icons in Photoshop because I thought two is enough for the first version. I made UI where I put my icons and I programmed that it blocks raycast through it that stops player moving to wrong spot.
